Professional Code of Conduct for Engineers Using AI to Create Automated Solutions

The recent rapid advancement of AI/LLM technology has introduced considerable confusion, disorientation, and with it alarming amounts of false promises, snake oil sales tactics, and sleazy shilling. Our duty as software professionals is to prevent such degradation of quality by insisting on a high watermark professional code of ethics.

ACG Manifesto

🚀

Embracing Innovation

We, software development professionals, are exploring the innovations made available by the development in the Artificial Intelligence space.

⚠️

Demanding Quality

We are concerned about the proliferation of half-baked, barely verified and tested content that current AI/LLM technology is notorious for delivering.

🛡️

Ensuring Safety & Security

We insist that a proper harness be provided to ensure engineering quality, safety, security, and legal liability for all products delivered with the assistance of AI technology.

🧠

Protecting Humans

We advocate the need for psychological safety of humans who are exposed to any products and content generated by AI.
