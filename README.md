> A professional guild for engineers who use AI to build real things — and refuse to pretend the tools are magic. We demand quality, ensure safety, and protect the humans in the loop. No worship. No hype. Just craft.

## What This Is

A professional guild for software engineers who use AI tools in their daily work. Not anti-AI. Not pro-hype. Pro-craft.

We hold ourselves to a **high watermark code of ethics** because the industry won't do it for us. We test what the model generates. We explain what we deploy. We protect the people who use what we build.

**Membership** means signing the manifesto. That's it. No fee, no certification, no gatekeeping.

## What This Is Not

| ❌ | ✅ |
|---|---|
| A company | A professional guild |
| A certification body | A shared code of conduct |
| A product | A set of principles |
| A gatekeeper | An open door |
| AI worship | AI craft |
