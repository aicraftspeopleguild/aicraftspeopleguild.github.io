Professional Code of Conduct for Engineers Using AI to Create Automated Solutions

The recent rapid advancement of AI/LLM technology has introduced considerable confusion, disorientation, and with it alarming amounts of false promises, snake oil sales tactics, and sleazy shilling. Our duty as software professionals is to prevent such degradation of quality by insisting on a high watermark professional code of ethics.
